## Abstract

This whitepaper presents an integrated theoretical framework connecting the Theory of Learning by Exception (TAE), Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), and the METFI model, which conceives Earth and collective cognition as toroidal electromagnetic systems driven by internal non-conservative forcing. Exceptions are formalized as structural singularities capable of inducing resonant recalibration in networks of coupled cognitive oscillators. The loss of toroidal symmetry is shown to generate non-linear transitions observable in geophysical, biological, and collective cognitive systems. The framework introduces non-conventional metrics, multiscale parametric forcing, and reproducible simulation pathways oriented toward structural validation rather than statistical optimization.
